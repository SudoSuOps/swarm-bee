The Bottleneck

The hidden reason AI projects fail

Most AI teams spend 60–80% of project time on data preparation. The model is rarely the problem. The data is.

Cleaning takes months

Raw data requires deduplication, normalization, validation, and formatting before it touches a training pipeline. Most teams underestimate this by 4–6x.

Synthetic data lacks trust

GPT-generated data introduces compounding errors. Without multi-model verification, synthetic pairs degrade downstream model performance instead of improving it.

Internal creation is expensive

Building domain-expert verified datasets in-house requires subject matter experts, annotation tooling, and months of iteration. Budget: $200K–$500K minimum.

Quality is invisible until deployment

Bad training data produces models that pass benchmarks but fail in production. The cost shows up in user churn, hallucination rates, and compliance risk.
