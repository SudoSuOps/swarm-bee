The Bottleneck

The hidden reason AI projects fail

Most AI teams spend 60–80% of project time on data preparation. The model is rarely the problem. The data is.

Cleaning takes months

Raw data requires deduplication, normalization, validation, and formatting before it touches a training pipeline. Most teams underestimate this by 4–6x.

Synthetic data lacks trust

GPT-generated data introduces compounding errors. Without multi-model verification, synthetic pairs degrade downstream model performance instead of improving it.

Internal creation is expensive

Building domain-expert verified datasets in-house requires subject matter experts, annotation tooling, and months of iteration. Budget: $200K–$500K minimum.

Quality is invisible until deployment

Bad training data produces models that pass benchmarks but fail in production. The cost shows up in user churn, hallucination rates, and compliance risk.

FAQ

Common questions

What format are the datasets delivered in?
JSONL (JSON Lines). Each line contains a structured object with question, answer, specialty, tier, source, and cove_score fields. Drop directly into Unsloth, Axolotl, TRL, LLaMA-Factory, or any custom SFT pipeline.
How are the datasets verified?
4-stage Chain-of-Verification (CoVe) pipeline. A 70B-parameter model generates each pair, then a 235B-parameter model independently verifies factual accuracy, format consistency, and domain correctness. Only pairs scoring 20+ out of 30 are promoted to platinum. 93.6% conversion rate. The 6.4% that fail are rejected or rewritten.
What does 30/30 benchmark mean?
We evaluate every dataset against 30 SFT suitability criteria: format consistency, answer completeness, clinical accuracy, specialty coverage, zero hallucination, proper attribution, and more. Our platinum data scores 30/30. No public dataset we've tested scores above 22/30.
What is the minimum purchase?
1,000 pairs at $99 for any single specialty. We also offer Pro (50K pairs, $499), Custom Build (100K pairs, $999), and Enterprise (250K pairs, $1,995). Free: 10 random pairs via our public API, no auth needed.
Do the datasets contain patient data or PII?
No. Zero patient data. Zero PII. All medical pairs are generated from authoritative textbooks and verified by our CoVe pipeline. Commercially licensable under Apache 2.0. Our PHI detection layer blocks 7 categories of identifiable data at generation time.
