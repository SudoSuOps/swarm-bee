The Market Problem

Aviation runs on information — and it's fragmented.

Airlines, MROs, regulators, manufacturers, and operators rely on massive volumes of:

  • Incident reports
  • Maintenance logs
  • Safety bulletins
  • Manuals and technical orders
  • Operational procedures
  • Regulatory guidance

Most of this information is:

  • Scattered across PDFs and legacy systems
  • Difficult to search and summarize
  • Time-consuming to analyze
  • Impossible to scale with human workflows alone
AI could help — but generic models are not trained for aviation.
That's where SwarmAviation fits.
