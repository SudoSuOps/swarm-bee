The Market Opportunity

Commercial real estate runs on judgment — and judgment doesn't scale.

Every CRE deal involves:

  • Cap rate analysis and valuation modeling
  • Tenant creditworthiness and lease structure
  • Market comps, absorption rates, and vacancy trends
  • Regulatory diligence, zoning, and entitlements
  • Operating expense benchmarks and NOI projections
  • Dark box analysis on vacant or underperforming assets

Most of this knowledge is:

  • Locked in the heads of senior brokers and underwriters
  • Scattered across CoStar, LoopNet, internal memos, and spreadsheets
  • Time-consuming to synthesize across deal types
  • Impossible to query at scale without domain-specific AI
Generic AI doesn't understand cap rates, tenant credit, or lease structures.
That's where Swarm-CRE fits.
Why CRE Needs Domain-Specific AI

CRE is not a consumer AI market.

It is a relationship-driven, data-heavy industry where:

  • Bad underwriting kills deals
  • Tenant vetting requires institutional knowledge
  • Market context changes by submarket and quarter
  • Dark box and value-add analysis requires pattern recognition

Generic LLMs don't know the difference between a 6-cap NNN and a 10-cap value-add.

We're building the data layer first.
