The Market Problem

Healthcare runs on clinical knowledge — and it's siloed.

Hospitals, health systems, researchers, and medtech companies depend on vast volumes of:

  • Clinical guidelines and treatment protocols
  • Diagnostic criteria and differential reasoning
  • Drug interactions and pharmacological data
  • Pathology reports and imaging interpretations
  • Board exam preparation and medical education
  • Regulatory compliance and documentation

Most of this knowledge is:

  • Locked in textbooks, journals, and institutional memory
  • Expensive to structure and maintain
  • Impossible to query at the point of care
  • Degraded by generic AI that hallucinates on clinical content
AI could transform clinical workflows — but generic models are not trained on authoritative medical content.
That's where SwarmMed fits.
Why Healthcare Needs Verified AI

Healthcare is not a consumer AI market.

It is a safety-critical industry where:

  • Hallucinations can harm patients
  • Traceability is a regulatory requirement
  • Data provenance must be auditable
  • Patient data can never leave the institution

Generic LLMs trained on Reddit and Wikipedia are not fit for clinical use.
